What It Does

When browsing insurance content, this extension highlights cost figures and lets you compare them against QuoteFii's government-sourced estimates. It presents both numbers side by side, with no judgment about which is "right."

Our Approach

QuoteFii's estimates are based on government data from the NAIC and BLS, not commercial data vendors. Different sources use different methodologies, so figures often differ. This extension helps you see those differences, not judge them.

Important: QuoteFii is not a licensed insurance provider or agent. All figures are estimates for informational purposes only, based on our analysis of publicly available government data. We do not claim our figures are more accurate than any other source. Differences between estimates may reflect different methodologies, coverage levels, time periods, or data sources. Always do your own research and speak with a qualified insurance provider for personalized quotes. See our sources and methodology.
